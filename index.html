Our Story

Not just coffee —
a ritual.

Grind & Gather was born from a simple obsession: finding the world's most extraordinary beans and doing them justice. Founded in 2018 by two friends who quit corporate life to chase the perfect cup, we've never looked back.

Every bag we roast carries the story of the farmer who grew it, the altitude that shaped it, and the hands that processed it. We believe coffee is the world's most democratic luxury — and we're here to share it.

  • 🌱

    Ethically Sourced

    Direct-trade partnerships with 14 farms across 9 countries.

  • 🔥

    Fresh Roasted

    Roasted on-site every morning. Never more than 5 days old.

  • ♻️

    Zero Waste

    Compostable packaging, spent grounds go to local gardens.

  • 🏆

    Award Winning

    Best Independent Roaster, City Coffee Awards 2023 & 2024.

Our Craft

From farm to your cup —
the journey matters.

  1. 🌍

    Origin & Sourcing

    We visit farms annually, tasting hundreds of lots to select beans that meet our strict quality and ethical standards.

  2. ⚖️

    Green Grading

    Every shipment is cupped and graded. Only SCA 85+ scoring beans make it to our drum roaster.

  3. 🔥

    Artisan Roasting

    Small 5kg batches on our vintage Probat drum roaster. Each profile is developed over weeks of iteration.

  4. ☕

    Your Perfect Cup

    Served within days of roasting by trained baristas who treat every drink as a finished dish.
